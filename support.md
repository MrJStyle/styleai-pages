## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I restore my purchases?**
Go to **Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases** to restore any previous StyleAI Plus or Pro subscription.

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

**What data does StyleAI collect?**
