# Privacy Policy

**StyleAI - Smart Closet**

*Last Updated: June 30, 2026*

## Introduction

StyleAI ("we," "our," or "us") operates the StyleAI - Smart Closet mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

- **Email address** — provided during registration or obtained from your Google/Apple account if you choose social sign-in.
- **Display name** — provided by you or obtained from your social sign-in profile.
- **Profile photo** — optionally uploaded by you or obtained from your social sign-in provider.
- **Authentication provider** — which sign-in method you used (email, Google, or Apple).

You may also use the App anonymously without providing personal information.

### 2. User-Generated Content

When you use the App's core features, you may provide:

- **Clothing photos** — images of fashion items you photograph or select from your photo library.
- **Wardrobe data** — item details such as name, category, color, brand, size, material, pattern, purchase date, price, store, ratings, and personal notes.
- **Outfit data** — outfit combinations, occasion tags, weather preferences, location descriptions, scheduled dates, and wear history.
- **Calendar entries** — dates associated with planned outfits and personal notes.

### 3. Location Data (Optional)

If you grant location permission, we collect:

- **GPS coordinates** (latitude and longitude) — used solely to fetch local weather forecasts to provide weather-appropriate outfit recommendations.
- **City name** — derived from your coordinates or manually selected by you.

Location access is entirely optional. You can deny or revoke location permission at any time through your device settings without affecting other App functionality.

### 4. Subscription & Purchase Data

When you subscribe to StyleAI Plus or Pro:

- **Subscription tier** — your current plan (Free, Plus, or Pro).
- **Usage credits** — the number of AI-powered features you have used within your billing period.

Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) and our subscription management partner, RevenueCat. We do **not** collect or store your credit card number, bank account details, or other financial payment information.

### 5. Automatically Collected Identifiers

- **User ID (UUID)** — a unique identifier generated by our authentication system to link your account across devices and services.

### 6. Information We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ We do **not** collect advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID).
- ❌ We do **not** use analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry SDKs (no Firebase, Sentry, Amplitude, Mixpanel, or similar services).
- ❌ We do **not** perform device fingerprinting.
- ❌ We do **not** track your activity across other apps or websites.

- **Purpose**: User authentication, database storage, file storage, and server-side processing.

- **Purpose**: Providing local weather forecasts for outfit recommendations.

### Apple & Google (Authentication)
- **Data shared**: OAuth tokens during sign-in.

## Data Storage & Security

### Where Your Data Is Stored

- **Cloud storage**: Your account data, wardrobe items, outfits, and images are stored on Supabase's cloud infrastructure to enable cross-device synchronization.
- **Local storage**: A copy of your data is also stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database for offline access.
- **Settings**: App preferences (theme, language, notification settings) are stored locally on your device only.

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) for all network communications.
- Row-level security policies on our database to ensure users can only access their own data.
- Authenticated API endpoints requiring valid user sessions.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

---

## Data Retention

- We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
- If you delete your account, we will delete your authentication records, profile data, and associated database records. Image files in cloud storage may be retained briefly for operational reasons before being purged.
- Local data on your device can be cleared by uninstalling the App or using the in-app data management features.

## Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access & Export
- You can view all your wardrobe items, outfits, and calendar data within the App.
- You can export your data using the App's built-in backup feature (Export as ZIP).

### Correction
- You can update your profile information, wardrobe items, and outfits at any time within the App.

### Deletion
- You can delete individual wardrobe items, outfits, or calendar entries at any time.
- You can delete your entire account through **Settings → Delete Account**. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data from our servers.
- For Apple Sign-In users, we also revoke your Apple token upon account deletion as required by Apple's guidelines.

### Location Permission
- You can grant or revoke location permission at any time through your device's system settings.

### Notifications
- You can manage notification preferences within the App settings or through your device's system settings.
